Welcome! This is the Gathering Space for the Planetary Analogs Sessions of AGU.

Is this your first time in a Gather Town Space? It is sort of like a Zoom call where you interact with folks nearby as you walk around like you are in a video game. If you have questions, feel free to find Jacob Richardson in the room and ask him. Below are some maps of how this room works:

You can walk around this area with your arrow keys. When you are close to people, you will be able to interact with them like a Zoom call.

Each e-Lightning presentation has its own space in this room. When you are near a poster space, you can hear only people at the same poster space. The AGU poster is not linked to in this room, but you can have the poster open in another tab or the presenter can share their screen near their own poster!

When you are in the public areas, you can hear and speak to anyone near you!
