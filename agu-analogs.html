Welcome!

This is the Gathering Space for the Planetary Analogs Sessions of AGU. Is this your first time in a Gather Town Space? It is sort of like a Zoom call where you interact with folks nearby as you walk around like you are in a video game. Here's a few maps!

You can walk around this area with your arrow keys. When you are close to people, you will be able to interact with them like a Zoom call.

When you are near a poster space, you can hear only people at the same poster space.

When you are in the public areas, you can hear and speak to anyone near you!
