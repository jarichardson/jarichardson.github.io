Jacob A. Richardson

Planetary Volcanologist

Bio

Jacob Richardson is a volcanologist who researches how volcanic terrains and magmatic processes evolve over geologic time. His study areas include volcanic regions on Earth and its neighboring bodies, Mars, Venus, and the Moon. Much of his work involves mapping, numerical modeling of lava flows, and statistical modeling.
